In order to take advantage of Veil Labs's software and services, users must abide by the following conditions laid out by the Veil Labs team.

## Use of Veil Labs software and Network

 - The use of Veil Labs software complies with any and all agreements pertaining to individual software use should such be present
 - The user acknowledges that user errors resulting in the loss of funds are not Veil Labs project's responsibility. Veil Labs staff will do their utmost to help recover lost funds should such events occur, but no guarantee is made that a user's lost funds can be returned
 - Veil Labs software should not under any circumstances be altered in an attempt to defraud the Veil Labs network, its users, or exchanges and services reliant on the Veil Labs network
 - Any and all uses of open source material pertaining to Veil Labs project software and source code must abide by the accompanying license and copyright notices. Further information on Veil Labs's license and copyright information can be found [here](/license/)

## Discord, Forums, and Social Media

 - Users are expected to abide by the rules set out in welcome messages where present
 - Users are expected to abide by the rules set out by the platform owner's themselves as outlined in the platform's terms of service
 - Users are expected to respect the rights and freedoms of other users, including their right to remain anonymous
 - Anyone attempting to scam, phish, or spread viruses will be dealt with by Veil Labs staff at the full extent of their power, including permanent bans and reporting to platform service operators
 - Anyone advertising or soliciting without first seeking prior permission will be warned and asked to suspend their activities. Failure to do so will result in a ban
 - Posting and sharing of material in breach of copyright, defamatory actions, and other unlawful behavior will not be tolerated

## Cloning and Forking of Veil Labs Source Code

 - Anyone cloning or forking Veil Labs's source code is expected to abide by licenses and copyright notices contained therein. Further information on Veil Labs's policy on clones and forks can be found [here](/license/)

## Participating in Veil Labs Bounty Program

 - Anyone participating in Veil Labs's bounty program cannot be involved in, or by formal association be directly linked to, the Veil Labs project in any way.
 - Anyone wishing to be paid a bounty is required to provide a valid Veil Labs address to which remittance will be paid.
 - Anyone taking on bounties acknowledges that the completion of a bounty is ultimately determined by a Veil Labs staff review, involving one or more staff dependent on the size of the bounty and task. This is to ensure the high standards the Veil Labs project holds itself to are maintained through any work contributed by bounty hunter s.
 
 Further information on the Veil Labs Bounty Program can be found [here](/get-started/)

# Use of Veil Labs Support Service

 - Users understand that Veil Labs staff will never assist them via direct message on Discord or other social media and forum services. All help will be given via Freshdesk or in-channel
 - Users are aware that they may be asked to provide information relevant to their problem in order to find a solution. This requested information will never include anything that jeopardizes the users account security or funds
 - Information may be retained for the sake of helping the user, improving Veil Labs software or services, or for building a database of solutions to better serve other users with similar issues. This will never include personal information or data on a user's funds

## False and Fraudulent Representation of Veil Labs or Veil Labs Staff

 - Anyone found to be misrepresenting Veil Labs by fraudulent representation of the Veil Labs brand or staff identities will be subject to any and all measures available to Veil Labs project representatives
